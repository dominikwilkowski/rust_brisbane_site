## Talks

### Bare Metal Meets Rust
A brief overview of the emerging role of Rust in bare-metal development, highlighting its core capabilities and exploring how its safety, memory model, and zero-cost abstractions align with the constraints and design philosophy of embedded systems.

– By [Brian](https://www.linkedin.com/in/brian-wu-91a3b9293/)

### 0–100 Making your CLI’s hum

The first 80% of a CLI is really just the first 20%. You get something working, ship it, and think you’re done — then the real work starts: installers, config, structured output, updates, release plumbing, and all the parts users quietly expect. 0–100 Making your CLI’s hum is a practical talk about that second phase, with lessons from building agentmark and cadence-cli.

– By [Chris Raethke](https://linkedin.com/in/chrisraethke)
